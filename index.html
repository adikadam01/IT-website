About Us

(Know us a bit more..!!)

Our Department of "Information Technology" is dedicated to fostering innovation and excellence in the rapidly evolving field of technology. With a focus on providing a comprehensive curriculum, we equip students with practical skills in programming, data analytics, networking, and emerging technologies. Our experienced faculty and state-of-the-art labs offer a dynamic learning environment, preparing students to excel in their careers and contribute meaningfully to the tech-driven world.

Events

Our Department hosts dynamic yearly events that foster innovation and creativity. The national level "CodeLite Hackathon" brings together tech enthusiasts to develop innovative solutions, showcasing coding prowess and teamwork. On the other hand, "Serendipity" is a vibrant cultural extravaganza that celebrates art, music, and talent, creating unforgettable experiences for participants and audiences alike. These events offer a perfect blend of technical brilliance and cultural expression, making every year memorable and inspiring.
